Formatting Rules 


  

    
Rule 1 · Main Title

    
The first title is replaced with <p><b>JUDGMENT</b></p> — bold, all caps.

  


  

    
Rule 2 · Paragraph Numbering

    
Main paragraph numbers like 1. or [3] become <b>[N]</b> with exactly one space before the text. Does not apply inside blockquotes.

  


  

    
Rule 3 · Section Headings

    
Standalone headings (e.g. BRIEF FACTUAL BACKGROUND) are wrapped in <SPARA_TITLE><p><b>...</b></p></SPARA_TITLE> and capitalised. Not applied inside blockquotes.

  


  

    
Rule 4 · Blockquotes

    
When a paragraph ends with :, -, or :-, subsequent paragraphs are wrapped in <blockquote> until a new main paragraph begins.

  


  

    
Rule 5 · List Spacing

    
Numbered/lettered list markers like (i)Word become (i) Word — exactly one space. No bolding of inner markers.

  


  

    
Rule 6 · Currency Format

    
All Malaysian Ringgit amounts are normalised to RMX,XXX.XX — no space after RM, thousands comma, always two decimal places. E.g. RM 7,000 → RM7,000.00, RM 6850 → RM6,850.00.

  


  

    
Rule 7 · Bullet Symbol

    
The bullet character • is replaced with its ASCII entity &#149; followed by exactly one space. E.g. •Left ear pinna → &#149; Left ear pinna.
