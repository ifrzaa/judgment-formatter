Formatting Rules 


  

    
Rule 1 · Main Title

    
The first title is replaced with <p><b>JUDGMENT</b></p> — bold, all caps.

  


  

    
Rule 2 · Paragraph Numbering

    
Main paragraph numbers like 1., 1 ., [3], or [ 3 ] become <b>[N]</b> with exactly one space before the text. Does not apply inside blockquotes.

  


  

    
Rule 3 · Section Headings

    
Standalone headings (e.g. Brief Factual Background) are wrapped in <b>...</b> with Title Case. Not applied inside blockquotes.

  


  

    
Rule 4 · Blockquotes

    
When a paragraph ends with :, -, or :-, subsequent paragraphs are wrapped in <blockquote> until a new main paragraph begins.

  


  

    
Rule 5 · List Spacing

    
Numbered/lettered list markers like (i)Word become (i) Word — exactly one space. No bolding of inner markers.

  


  

    
Rule 6 · Title Recognition

    
If the first paragraph is AWARD or Award, it becomes <p><b>AWARD</b></p>. If it is ALASAN PENGHAKIMAN, that exact title is used bold instead.

  


  

    
Rule 7 · Paragraph Number Formats

    
All variants are normalised to <b>[N]</b>: 1. text, 1 . text, [1] text, and [ 1 ] text are all recognised.

  


  

    
Rule 8 · Reference Block

    
A REFERENCE: section found before the title is automatically moved to appear immediately after the title, rendered as <p><b>Reference: </b></p> followed by its body paragraph.

  


  

    
Rule 9 · Emphasis Added

    
Any occurrence of (emphasis added), [emphasis added], or [Emphasis added] (any casing) is normalised to [Emphasis Added].
